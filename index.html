FAQ

Questions doctors usually ask.

Is NutriCopilot for doctors or patients?

NutriCopilot is designed for doctors, dietitians, and clinical nutrition teams. It is not a consumer food diary or calorie tracker.

Does AI automatically approve diet plans?

No. AI can generate a draft recommendation, but the doctor reviews, edits, and approves the final clinical diet plan.

Can doctors edit recommendations?

Yes. Recommendations are designed to be reviewed and modified before approval.

Does it work with uploaded reports?

The prototype showcases report upload, biomarker extraction, risk flag review, and plan generation flows.

Can weekly diet plans be generated?

Yes. NutriCopilot is positioned around doctor-editable weekly clinical diet plans and meal schedules.

Is this a replacement for clinical judgment?

No. It is an AI-assisted workflow tool. Clinical judgment and final approval remain with the doctor.
